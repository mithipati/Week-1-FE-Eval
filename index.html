Tell me more about Hoagie Haven

There's no better place to get a hoagie. Spices? SaltPeppaKetchup?...Next, next in line. Where good friends meet to eat...mmmm, this is good.

Going Back

to the early 1970's, Hoagie Haven was the first to serve up large hoagies in the Princeton area. With help from Italian People's Bakery, Uncle Haven demanded a larger and larger roll to fill with delicious foods. Finally, when the roll couldn't fit on the counter, they knew it was big enough. By keeping it in the family, Uncle Haven's nephews, Mike, Niko, and Costa Maltabes are now running the business.
